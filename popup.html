Help Center

How do I create a new account plan?

Click on the "Create" tab, enter your account name, and click "Create Plan". A new Google Sheet will be created with your account plan template. The plan will open automatically after creation, or you can find it later in the "Manage" tab.

Can I modify an existing plan?

Yes! Go to the "Manage" tab and click "Open" on the plan you wish to edit. This will open the Google Sheet where you can make changes to any section including account profile, executive summary, and strategic overview.

How do I delete a plan?

In the "Manage" tab, find the plan you want to remove and click the "Delete" button. You'll be asked to confirm before the plan is deleted. Note that this permanently removes the sheet from your Google Drive.

Where are my plans stored?

All your account plans are stored in a master Google Sheet in your Google Drive. Each plan is a separate tab within that spreadsheet. The spreadsheet is only accessible to you and anyone you explicitly share it with.

How do I search for a specific plan?

In the "Manage" tab, use the search box at the top to filter your plans by name. Simply start typing and results will appear instantly. Click the "X" icon to clear your search and show all plans again.

Can I use keyboard shortcuts?

Yes! RevPilot AI offers several keyboard shortcuts to improve your productivity:

  • Alt+C: Switch to Create tab
  • Alt+M: Switch to Manage tab
  • Alt+N: Focus on new plan form
  • Alt+D: Toggle dark mode
  • Alt+H: Open this help popup

You can see all shortcuts in the "Shortcuts" tab of this help center.

What's included in each account plan?

Each account plan contains several structured sections:

  • Account Profile: Basic account information, industry details, revenue, etc.
  • Executive Summary: Current situation, key pain points, value proposition, and success metrics
  • Strategic Overview: Business challenges, goals, solutions, and success criteria
  • Key Contacts: Stakeholder information with roles and contact details

You can view the full list of included features by clicking the "What's included?" button on the Create tab.

How do I switch between dark and light mode?

Click the moon icon in the top-left corner of the extension to toggle between dark and light mode. You can also use the keyboard shortcut Alt + D. Your preference will be saved for future sessions.

How can I refresh my plans list?

If you've made changes to your plans outside the extension or need to refresh the list for any reason, you can click the "Refresh Plans" button at the bottom of the Manage tab. This will fetch the latest data from your Google Drive.

Privacy Policy

At RevPilot AI, we take your privacy seriously. Here's what you should know about how our extension handles your data:

Data Collection

This extension only accesses and stores data necessary for its functionality:

  • Account plan information you create
  • Google authentication tokens (for accessing Google Sheets)
  • User preferences (like dark mode setting)
Data Storage

Your data is stored in:

  • Chrome's local storage (on your device only)
  • Your Google Drive (account plans as spreadsheets)

We never store your data on our servers.

Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • Google Sheets & Drive access: To create and manage your account plans
  • Storage: To save your preferences and recent plans
Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with Google services to provide functionality. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how they handle your data.

Data Removal

If you uninstall the extension:

  • Local storage data will be removed from your device
  • Spreadsheets created in your Google Drive will remain unless you delete them manually
